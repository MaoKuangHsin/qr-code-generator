Hi there! I'm Cindy.

The original objective behind building this QR code generator was to have a scalable and privacy-first solution — because I didn't want to be tracked by any third-party websites.

All QR code generation happens entirely in your browser, locally — no information is uploaded or shared externally.

This is entirely a frontend solution. You can monitor your network console to verify that the QR generation process only loads a DataURL image and does not interact with any backend servers.

No cookies, no sessions, no external servers involved. Feel free to inspect everything through your browser console!
